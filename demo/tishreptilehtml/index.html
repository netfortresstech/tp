Reptile Facts Ticker

Did you know turtles have existed for over 200 million years?
Snakes can eat prey larger than their diameter due to their jaw flexibility.
Lizards can communicate through body language.
Some reptiles like the alligator have a powerful bite force.
Geckos can climb smooth surfaces due to microscopic hairs on their toes.
The Komodo dragon is the largest living lizard species.
Many reptiles lay eggs, but some, like boas, give live birth.
Reptiles are cold-blooded and regulate their temperature externally.
The longest snake ever recorded was a reticulated python at 32 feet.
Crocodiles have a sophisticated social structure and communication system.
