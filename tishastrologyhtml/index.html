Zodiac Sign Profiles

♈ Aries

Aries is the first sign of the zodiac, and is represented by the ram. Aries are known for being passionate, energetic...

Aries are known for being passionate, energetic, and independent. They are also known for being impulsive and sometimes reckless.

  • Strengths: Courageous, determined, enthusiastic
  • Weaknesses: Impatient, impulsive, aggressive
  • Compatibility: Leo, Sagittarius

♉ Taurus

Taurus is the second sign of the zodiac, and is represented by the bull ...

Taurus are known for being reliable, stable, and sensual. They are also known for being stubborn and materialistic.

  • Strengths: Patient, reliable, practical
  • Weaknesses: Stubborn, materialistic, possessive
  • Compatibility: Virgo, Capricorn

♊ Gemini

Gemini is the third sign of the zodiac, and is represented by the twins ...

Gemini are known for being communicative, adaptable, and intelligent. They are also known for being superficial and indecisive.

  • Strengths: Communicative, adaptable, intelligent
  • Weaknesses: Superficial, indecisive, restless
  • Compatibility: Libra, Aquarius

♋ Cancer

Cancer is the fourth sign of the zodiac, and is represented by the crab ...

Cancer are known for being emotional, intuitive, and caring. They are also known for being moody and insecure.

  • Strengths: Emotional, intuitive, caring
  • Weaknesses: Moody, insecure, manipulative
  • Compatibility: Scorpio, Pisces

♌ Leo

Leo is the fifth sign of the zodiac, and is represented by the lion ...

Leo are known for being confident, dramatic, and creative. They are also known for being self-centered and demanding.

  • Strengths: Confident, dramatic, creative
  • Weaknesses: Self-centered, demanding, arrogant
  • Compatibility: Aries, Sagittarius

♍ Virgo

Virgo is the sixth sign of the zodiac, and is represented by the virgin ...

Virgo are known for being practical, analytical, and hardworking. They are also known for being critical and perfectionistic.

  • Strengths: Practical, analytical, hardworking
  • Weaknesses: Critical, perfectionistic, indecisive
  • Compatibility: Taurus, Capricorn

♎ Libra

Libra is the seventh sign of the zodiac, and is represented by the scales ...

Libra are known for being fair, diplomatic, and charming. They are also known for being indecisive and superficial.

  • Strengths: Fair, diplomatic, charming
  • Weaknesses: Indecisive, superficial, flaky
  • Compatibility: Gemini, Aquarius

♏ Scorpio

Scorpio is the eighth sign of the zodiac, and is represented by the scorpion ...

Scorpio are known for being passionate, intense, and secretive. They are also known for being jealous and manipulative.

  • Strengths: Passionate, intense, intuitive
  • Weaknesses: Jealous, manipulative, secretive
  • Compatibility: Cancer, Pisces

♐ Sagittarius

Sagittarius is the ninth sign of the zodiac, and is represented by the archer ...

Sagittarius are known for being optimistic, adventurous, and philosophical. They are also known for being blunt and tactless.

  • Strengths: Optimistic, adventurous, philosophical
  • Weaknesses: Blunt, tactless, restless
  • Compatibility: Aries, Leo

♑ Capricorn

Capricorn is the tenth sign of the zodiac, and is represented by the goat ...

Capricorn are known for being ambitious, responsible, and disciplined. They are also known for being materialistic and cynical.

  • Strengths: Ambitious, responsible, disciplined
  • Weaknesses: Materialistic, cynical, stubborn
  • Compatibility: Taurus, Virgo

♒ Aquarius

Aquarius is the eleventh sign of the zodiac, and is represented by the water bearer ...

Aquarius are known for being independent, original, and humanitarian. They are also known for being aloof and detached.

  • Strengths: Independent, original, humanitarian
  • Weaknesses: Aloof, detached, idealistic
  • Compatibility: Gemini, Libra

♓ Pisces

Pisces is the twelfth and final sign of the zodiac, and is represented by the fish ...

Pisces are known for being compassionate, intuitive, and dreamy. They are also known for being indecisive and escapist.

  • Strengths: Compassionate, intuitive, dreamy
  • Weaknesses: Indecisive, escapist, self-pitying
  • Compatibility: Cancer, Scorpio
